About Us

Delivering Innovative IT Solutions!

Jecmas was founded by Paul Eshun In 2017, after a successful development, installation and implementation of a savings & loans monitoring application for Rosefos Enterprise in Koforidua which he partnered with Eric Akompong as a final year project work. He later developed the passion for application programming and started Jecmas as a web design and software development company.

Jecmas Solutions started with an ultimate aim of developing software solutions which can easily scale to fulfill the needs of startups and smaller businesses.
Since we started, we have worked on several projects ranging from websites, to desktops & web applications, and mobile apps as well as digital solutions (video editing).

Since 2017, Jecmas has been delivering full cycle software development services to schools, hospitals, churches, ngo's, businesses and organizations. We have provided custom software development, web design, graphic design, digital solutions, IT training and consulting services to numerous customers.

Over the years, Jecmas has grown to be an expert in designing, development, customization and integration of complex enterprise-level solutions, advanced web and mobile applications offering a well-balanced blend of technology skills, domain knowledge, hands-on experience, effective methodology and passion for IT.

We are a young, energetic, passionate and committed team with great astuteness in delivering customer tailored products.
